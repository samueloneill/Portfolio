About Me

I have had a strong interest in electronics since my early teens, which was the reason behind my choice to study electronic engineering at university. I studied at the University of Portsmouth, and graduated in 2021 with a 2:1 Bachelor's degree.
In my time at university I discovered a passion for product design through individual and group design projects. While I specialise in embedded software development, I enjoy spending time working on any part of a project from conception, to design, to implementation. Coming up with new ideas and bringing them to reality is my passion.
